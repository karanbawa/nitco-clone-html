About Us

Since our inception in 1992, we have remained loyal to our Core values

"Innovation, Product quality and Customer service"

Our History

In the year 1994 the organization shifted its core business from marbles to tiles because of larger demand and volume growth in tiles. Subsequently our organization became one of the key partners of Kajaria Ceramics Ltd. In 2004, we became the sole distributer for some of the leading tiles brands in India.
We reached another major milestone in the year 2007, when we became the sole distributer in the entire Delhi region for multiple large tile companies like Italica, Varmora, and Sunheart. As our business grew, we were able to expand to various regions of NCR like Gurgaon, Faridabad, Ghaziabad and Noida by 2008.

Through hard work, dedication and expertise in our field, we were able to significantly expand our dealer network to complete North Indian region by 2016. In the year 2018 we were able to spread our wings and create a strong dealer network to other parts of India as well.

The turning point for us came in the year 2018, when we decided to get into manufacturing and launch our own flagship brand ZEBBOI. Zebboi offers complete tiling solution to both B2B and B2C customers. With innovative designs, Zebboi plans to have a Pan India presence and focus on the growing export market as well.

Strong National Network

We have a strong network within the country. Our manufacturing unit is located in Morbi, Gujarat. We also have showrooms and warehouses in various locations that helps us in serving our customers better. We plan to expand our operations in other parts of the country as well.
