    Buy Designer Glazed Vitrified Tiles at NITCO

    Glazed vitrified tiles are durable and high on functionality. Due to their shine, they also look aesthetically pleasing. They can easily enhance the appeal of any room as they come in various designs, finishes, and textures like wood, stone, slate and more. Vitrified tiles are usually used for flooring as they are highly impervious and available in large sizes.

    How To Select The Right Glazed Vitrified Tiles

    There is a wide range of glazed vitrified tiles that are available in the market. Therefore, make sure you follow the right tips before deciding on the perfect tiles for your space:

    • Select tiles from a known brand :

      It is always advisable to pick your tiles from a reputed brand. At NITCO, we have been experts in this field for decades. Our experts can guide you with suitable tiles based on your colour theme and budget.

    • Check technical specifications :

      Vitrified tiles are very dense, and consequently, non-porous. Hence ensure that you check the technical specifications of the tile related to water absorption. Tiles that absorb less water are more robust and durable.

    • Select the preferred size of tile :

      The standard sizes of vitrified tiles available in the market are 600x600, 800x800, 600x1200, 800x1600 mm or millimeter. You can also choose smaller tile sizes like 300x300, 300x450, 300x600 for the bedroom. Additionally, as NITCO's living room vitrified tiles have fewer visible joint lines, they can make your home look spacious. Moreover, NITCO tiles boast of features like anti-skid, anti-bacterial, chemical resistant and also scratch and stain resistant.

    • Choose the pattern and colour:

      Glazed vitrified tiles are available in diverse designs, colours, patterns and prints. Choose light-coloured tiles to give an airy look to your house.

    Frequently Asked Questions on Glazed Vitrified Tiles

    • What are glazed vitrified tiles?

      Glazed Vitrified Tiles are made from a glazed coating applied to vitrified tiles. Glazing assists in offering an array of designs and textures on the tiles.

    • Are glazed vitrified tiles slippery?

      Polished glazed vitrified tiles are slippery whereas unpolished glazed vitrified tiles are slip resistant. In fact, NITCO has a range of anti-skid tiles that can prevent from any hazard.

    • What is the life of glazed vitrified tiles?

      Glazed vitrified tiles are highly durable and can comfortably last for years. They are also easy to maintain, reducing the chances of them wearing out early.

    • Which is better, polished glazed vitrified tiles or glazed vitrified tiles?

      Polished glazed vitrified tiles are super glossy and also come in a variety of designs and patterns. They go through an additional firing process that makes them less slip-resistant and scratch-resistant compared to unpolished glazed vitrified tiles.

    • What are glazed vitrified tiles used for?

      Glazed vitrified tiles are used for flooring as well as wall applications. Depending on the design and texture you choose, you can pick the right tiles for your space.

    • What is the difference between polished glazed vitrified tiles and unpolished glazed vitrified tiles?

      Polished GVT are super-glossy and are available in a variety of styles and designs making them ideal for interiors, whereas unpolished GVT are denser, thicker and more durable, making them ideal for the outdoors. Unpolished GVT are also excellent at being slip resistant and a great choice for kitchen and bathroom floors.
